OUR SERVICES

Geophysical Services:

In collaboration with leading geophysical companies, we provide acquisition, processing, and
interpretation of 2D and 3D seismic data in offshore, onshore, and transit zones.

Drilling and Support Services

We offer efficient and reliable drilling and support services to facilitate seamless operations for oil and gas companies.

Production Related Services:

Our production-related services encompass a wide range of offerings, including coil tubing
and hydrofracturing, to enhance and optimize production processes.

Supply of Oil and Gas Equipment:

We also specialize in the supply of high-quality equipment required for oil and gas
exploration and production activities.

Renewable Energy

Within the Nityam Energy division, we are actively involved in the field of renewable energy.
We focus on two key areas

Tidal and Offshore Wind Energy

We are committed to exploring and harnessing the potential of tidal and offshore wind
energy as a sustainable power source.

Production, storage & Transportation of Hydrogen

We actively contribute to the production and efficient transportation of hydrogen, a clean and renewable energy resource.

OUR CONSULTANCY DIVISION
NITYAM SOLUTION
Consultancy has been one of our core business offerings since the inception of Nityam
Resources Pvt Limited. Under this division, we provide comprehensive services related to
foreign companies venturing into the oil and gas exploration sector in India.

Consulting Services

Our consultancy services include the following. Given the unique requirements and licenses associated with each project in India, we encourage you to reach out to us for more detailed information.

Incorporation of Company

We assist foreign companies in establishing their presence in India, guiding them through
the incorporation process and ensuring compliance with all necessary regulations.

Taxation and Compliance Consultancy

We provide expert advice and guidance regarding taxation and other compliance
requirements, ensuring that our clients adhere to the relevant laws and regulations.

Licensing and Support Services

Navigating the complex landscape of licenses and project-specific requirements can be challenging. We provide the necessary support to obtain all required licenses and support
services to ensure successful project completion.
